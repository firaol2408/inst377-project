Advanced Stat Explanations

PER

Player Efficiency Rating measures a player's overall per-minute productivity. League average is typically 15.

VORP

Value Over Replacement Player estimates how much value a player provides compared to a replacement-level player.

BPM

Box Plus Minus estimates a player's impact on team performance per 100 possessions.

TS%

True Shooting Percentage measures scoring efficiency, including free throws and three-pointers.

Win Shares

Win Shares estimates the number of team wins contributed by a player throughout a season.
